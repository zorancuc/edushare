At the core of EduShare services is our public blockchain platform. The decentralized nature of our public blockchain will grant accessibility to whoever seeking to study or wanting to help others study.

Edushare is developing to be a global education sharing platform where you can find tutors and tutees in your own neighborhood for any given subject. Moreover, institutions can also use Edushare to assist students or find potential tutors with the right talent.

EduShare tokens (EDS) can be used on this platform to search and contact potential tutors/tutees and check their resume as well, just to make sure that they are who you are looking for. Users can make secure payments without having to worry about being scammed.
